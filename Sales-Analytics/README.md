# 📈 Sales Performance Dashboard

## 📊 Overview

This Power BI dashboard analyzes sales performance across products, regions, and time periods to provide insights into revenue growth, profitability, and business performance trends.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Track revenue and profit performance
- Identify top-performing products and regions
- Monitor sales trends over time
- Compare performance against targets
- Improve business decision-making

---

## 📌 Key Metrics

- Total Revenue
- Total Profit
- Profit Margin
- Total Orders
- Average Order Value

---

## 📊 Visualizations

- Monthly Sales Trend
- Revenue by Region
- Top 10 Products by Sales
- Customer Contribution Analysis
- Profit vs Target Comparison
- Sales Distribution by Category

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

- Power BI
- DAX
- Power Query
- Excel
