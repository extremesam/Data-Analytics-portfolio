# 💰 Financial Analytics Dataset — Power BI Ready

A 24-month financial dataset built for revenue analysis, budget tracking, departmental spending, and executive KPI reporting.

---

## 📁 Workbook Structure (4 Sheets)

| Sheet | Purpose |
|-------|---------|
| **Financial Data** | 288 raw transaction rows — revenue & expenses across all months, departments, and regions |
| **Monthly P&L Summary** | 24-month profit & loss with actuals vs budget for every KPI |
| **Dept Spending Analysis** | Per-department total spend, budget, and variance summary |
| **KPI Summary** | Executive-level snapshot of all headline numbers |

---

## 📊 Dataset Specs

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| 📅 Time Period | January 2023 – December 2024 (24 months) |
| 🏢 Departments | Sales, Marketing, Operations, HR, IT, Finance, Logistics, R&D |
| 🌍 Regions | Lagos, Abuja, Port Harcourt, Kano |
| 💰 Total Revenue | $29.1M |
| 💸 Total Expenses | $16.6M |
| 📈 Net Profit | $12.5M |
| 🎯 Avg Profit Margin | 42.7% |

---

## 📺 Power BI Visualization Mapping

| Visual | Source Sheet | Fields |
|--------|-------------|--------|
| Revenue vs Expenses Trend | Monthly P&L Summary | Revenue + Expenses columns by Month |
| Profit Over Time | Monthly P&L Summary | Net Profit by Month |
| Expense Breakdown by Category | Financial Data | Sub-Category filter on Expense type |
| Budget vs Actual Comparison | Financial Data + Monthly P&L Summary | Actual vs Budget + Variance columns |
| Departmental Spending Analysis | Dept Spending Analysis | Direct plug-in — no transformation needed |

---

## 🎨 Color Coding

| Indicator | Meaning |
|-----------|---------|
| 🟢 Green variance | Positive variance (under budget / above target) |
| 🔴 Red variance | Negative variance (over budget / below target) |

> Variance columns are pre-color-coded for quick scanning directly in Excel — no conditional formatting setup required.
