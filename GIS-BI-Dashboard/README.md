# 🌍 GIS + ESG Analytics Dashboard

## 📊 Overview

This project combines Geographic Information Systems (GIS) and business intelligence to analyze urban heat distribution, green space accessibility, and environmental inequality in Lagos.

It supports ESG (Environmental, Social, and Governance) decision-making and urban planning.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Map urban heat intensity zones
- Identify green space distribution
- Analyze population exposure to heat
- Highlight environmental inequality
- Support sustainable urban planning

---

## 📌 Key Metrics

- Surface Temperature Index
- Green Cover Percentage
- Population Exposure Index
- Vulnerability Score
- Land Use Distribution

---

## 📊 Visualizations

- Heat Map of Urban Areas
- Green Space Distribution Map
- Vulnerability Index Map
- Land Use Classification
- Population vs Heat Exposure Chart

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

- Power BI (Map Visuals)
- QGIS / ArcGIS
- Remote Sensing Data
- Excel
- Raster & Vector Data Processing

## 📈 Key Insights

- High heat intensity is concentrated in densely built areas
- Green space is unevenly distributed across the city
- Vulnerable populations are more exposed to heat stress
- Urban planning gaps contribute to environmental inequality
